—since the glow is still there, it means the CSS that kills the halo isn’t winning over the component’s own hover/focus styles (usually DaisyUI/Tailwind “card / ring / shadow” utilities). Let’s force it to win without guessing class names.

Below is a single, stronger override that (1) does not depend on any custom class and (2) targets the most common tile elements (div.card, button, a, and [role="button"]) including their :hover / :focus / :focus-visible states.

What to do (one paste to your codebase)

Open (or create) client/src/styles/quick-access.css and replace the content with the rules below (or paste these at the very end of the file).

#quick-access-modal,
