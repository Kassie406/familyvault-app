🎨 Color Style Guide

Primary: Gold (#D4AF37 or #FFD700)

Secondary: Black (#000000)

Backgrounds: Off-white (#F8F8F8) or very dark gray (#111111)

Accents: White (#FFFFFF) for text contrast, subtle gray (#E0E0E0) for dividers
